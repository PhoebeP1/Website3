1. Pumpkin Patch!

Go to any pumpkin patch! Feast yourself with apple cider donuts, carmel apples, kettle corn, and more. Don't forget to wear your mask out in public!

2. Carve Pumpkins!

Buy pumpkins at the pumpkin patch or at your local Jewel. Either free hand your design or print out on paper and outline with something sharp into the pumpkin. (Save your pumpkin seeds for later!)

3. Bake Pumpkin Seeds!

After cleaning the seeds off with water, throw them on some tin foil on a tray to bake in the oven. I put cinnamon sugar, salt, and olive oil before baking. Then sprinkle some pure sugar after too for an extra sweet taste.

4. Costume/Makeup

Go thrifting or shopping to create either a unique costume or lookalike of something else! Micheals, amazon.com, and thrift stores will be usueful places in building your costume.

5. Movies!

Rent or find free movies off local streaming platforms for some festive horror movies and annimations such as The Nightmare Before Christmas, Coraline, Paranorman, Corpse Bride, and many more!
